
# The Problem
Undergraduates face obstacles when seeking research opportunities in niche technology fields, preventing them from gaining research expertise and robbing these fields of valuable new talent.

# Our Solution
We offer a web service called Research Custodian that connects students and research labs. Students fill out a profile to indicate their research interests, then they are to complete a portfolio project to display in their application.
The labs will define broad project topics for students to choose from. The students can use the completed projects to apply to research and connect with experts in their niche fields of interest.

For students, Research Custodian makes the niche technology space easier to navigate, with clearly defined steps and prerequisite tasks during the research application process.

For researchers, Research Custodian streamlines the search for highly motivated research assistants.

## Run Locally

**Prerequisites:**  Node.js


1. Install dependencies:
   `npm install`
2. Set the `GEMINI_API_KEY` in [.env.local](.env.local) to your Gemini API key
3. Run the app:
   `npm run dev`
